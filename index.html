About Us

Garments Solution Bangladesh, a Bangladesh-based company with global footprint, others steady, sustainable and enduring development as a provider of certifcation, assessment, audit, verifcation, inspection and capacity building. GSB aspires to be aligned with the world goal of sustainable development over the globe. Specializing in the areas of Product Certifcations, Labor and Human Rights auditing, Circular Economy, Envronmental Assessments and Management System Certifctions, GSB focuses onproviding professional, impartial and objective services to organizations of all sizes, in all industries, in any occation to help them manage their sustainable development.

Garments Solution Bangladesh is currently working with 500+ operators and organizations over the world to corroborate social fairness in labor condition, ethical & responsible business practice, intrinsic and integral management system, organic consumption, sustainability, hazardous chemical free production, circular economy, potential reduction in consumption of natural resources, environmental up-gradation, so that a world livable can be built by highly competent and advanced professional individuals.
